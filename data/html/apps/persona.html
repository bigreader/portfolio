Persona connects your favorite movies and TV shows in a unique way by tracking the actors you see and highlighting ones you know. Don’t be stuck wondering where you’ve seen that person before – Persona has you covered.

Build your library by adding movies and TV shows you’ve seen, and Persona will sort through the cast and crew to find and highlight anyone you’ve seen before. With over half a million movies and TV shows and 1.5 million people to choose from, you’ll find anything you watch.

See all the people you know in a particular movie, view your most-seen actors with number of roles you know, or see who an actor works with the most. Whatever you watch, Persona can show you a whole new side.

Persona is free to download and use. Data and images come from TheMovieDB, a free editable database for movies and TV shows. Can’t find your favorite movie? You can add it yourself at themoviedb.org.
